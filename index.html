About Vex

A Rivals clan created for people who want clean stacked lobbies and a solid team to improve with.

Competitive but chill

Mindset

We like winning, but we also like having fun. Trash talk is fine as long as it is friendly and doesn’t cross lines. Play serious, respect teammates and keep the vibes good.

Clear comms

Teamplay

Callouts, info and quick decisions matter more than ego. Vex squads focus on short, clear comms so everyone knows what is happening in every fight.

Room to grow

Progress

Whether you are learning Rivals or already cracked, there is space to improve here: VOD reviews, tips from better players, and scrims/tryouts as the clan grows.

Join Vex

Getting in is simple. Just be active, respect others and actually play Rivals.

How to join

  1. Click the Join Discord button or link below.
  2. Read the rules channel so you know what is allowed.
  3. Say hi in hi-and-bye and tell people your rank and role.
  4. Queue ranked in the right channel and start stacking.

Basic expectations

  • Use comms in games where it matters.
  • No cheating, account sharing or weird behaviour.
  • Respect staff decisions – owners have the final call.
  • Help newer players if you are more experienced.
